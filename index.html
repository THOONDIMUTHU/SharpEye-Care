TREATMENTS & SERVICES

Complete Eye Care and Optometry

Vision Correction Treatments

Prescription Glasses and Contact Lenses:For refractive errors like myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and presbyopia.

Implantable Contact Lenses: For those unsuitable for laser surgery.

Refractive Surgery: Reshapes the cornea to correct vision.

Implantable Contact Lenses: For those unsuitable for laser surgery

Cataract Treatments

Cataract Surgery:Removal of the cloudy lens and replacement with an intraocular lens (IOL).

Multi Lenses: Options include monofocal, multifocal, and toric lenses.

Photorefractive Keratectomy: An alternative for patients with thin corneas.

Small Incision Lenticule Extraction: Minimally invasive refractive surgery.

Glaucoma Treatments

Medications:Eye drops or oral medications to lower intraocular pressure (IOP).

Laser Procedures: Improves drainage in open-angle glaucoma.

Surgical Interventions:Devices like the Ahmed or Baerveldt shunts manage

Iridotomy: Creates a small hole in the iris.

Advanced Eye Care and Optometry

Retina Treatments

Diabetic Retinopathy:Intravitreal injections of anti-VEGF drugs (e.g., Avastin, Lucentis) to reduce swelling.

Retinal Detachment: Laser surgery or cryopexy to reattach the retina.

Refractive Surgery: Removes the vitreous gel and repairs the retina.

Macular Degeneration: Anti-VEGF injections for wet AMD.

Corneal Treatments

Corneal Transplants:Full-thickness or partial-thickness.

Keratoconus Management: Collagen cross-linking to strengthen the cornea.

Treatment for Corneal Infections:Antimicrobial medications and surgical repairs.

Implantable Contact Lenses: Custom contact lenses or implantable rings.

Pediatric Eye Treatments

Prescription Glasses and Contact Lenses:For refractive errors like myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and presbyopia.

Amblyopia (Lazy Eye): Patching therapy or vision exercises.

Strabismus: Corrective surgery for misaligned eyes.

Congenital Cataracts or Glaucoma:Early surgical intervention.

Premier Eye Care and Optometry

Oculoplastic Treatments

Eyelid Surgery:For ptosis , entropion or ectropion.

Tear Duct Surgery:DCR to clear blockages.

Orbital Surgery: Reshapes the cornea to correct vision.

Cosmetic Enhancements: blepharoplasty for aesthetic improvements.

Eye Infections Treatments

Conjunctivitis:Antibacterial, antiviral, or anti-inflammatory medications.

Uveitis: Corticosteroids or immunosuppressants.

Keratitis: Antibiotic or antifungal eye drops.

Neuro-Ophthalmic: Treatment for optic nerve disorders, double vision, and vision.

Advanced Treatments

Stem Cell Therapy:For degenerative conditions like macular degeneration.

Gene Therapy: For hereditary retinal disorders.

Emergency Immediate care for injuries, chemical burns, sudden vision loss, or acute conditions .

Artificial Retinas: Like retinal implants for severe vision loss.
